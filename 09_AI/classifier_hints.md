
# Classifier Hints

Use these hints to tag images or prompts with branch identities. Look for specific pattern behaviours and world elements rather than generic psychedelic cues.

**Classic BW Retinal Op:** Severe monochrome, crisp geometry, high repetition, no props.
**Radial Hypnosis Fields:** Centre pull, rosettes or spokes, eye/sun motifs, ceremonial feel.
**Stripe-Fluid Distortion:** Wavy stripes, fluid membranes, hidden forms, bodily flow.
**Chromatic Interference Op:** Black-and-white skeleton with spectral fringing or blacklight charge.
**Op Landscapes:** Terrain surfaces built from contour or zebra patterns; horizon sun or target.
**Op Fashion & Bodysuits:** Figures wearing pattern garments; body and environment share pattern logic.
**Anatomical Op Mapping:** Bodies wrapped with contour or stripe patterns; anatomy as pattern.
**Op Portraits:** Faces integrated with halos, contour skins, prismatic edges; strong gaze.
**Eye-Object Iconography:** Repeating eyes in objects, mushrooms, suns; watchful ecologies.
**Plush Candy Optical Worlds:** Soft, plush, toy-like species anchored by severe patterns; cute/eerie tone.
**Pattern Passageways:** Corridors or rooms built from pattern; route or threshold logic.
**Psychedelic Material Contamination:** Glossy, marbled, oily surfaces overlaying patterns; colour bloom.
**Op Editorial / Glam Theater:** Staged glam scenes with patterns integrated into set, wardrobe, and props.

If an image cannot be associated with one of these hints, or if it contains generic rainbows, swirls, or unrelated psychedelic props, it may not belong to the style.
