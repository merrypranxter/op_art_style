
# Lips

Lips are soft, sensual, symbolic objects in Retinal Surrealism. They can function as glossy relics, candy icons, mouth‑portals, glam accents, or repeating species.

**Modes:**
- **Glam Lip Relic:** Highly polished, synthetic mouth used as prop or altar object.
- **Candy Lip:** Bright, toy‑like, playful mouth used in plush worlds.
- **Mouth Portal:** Lips framing a hole or opening, suggesting entry or consumption.
- **Repeating Lip Species:** Lips repeated as an ecology of symbols.
- **Gloss-Contaminated Lip:** Lips invaded by prismatic bloom or oil‑slick sheen.

**Uses:** Place lips as focal objects, route markers, glam icons, or part of an eye‑mouth symbolic system. Keep them anchored to the world geometry through halo rings or contour mapping. Avoid random sexy lip stickers with no structural integration.
