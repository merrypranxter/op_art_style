
# Mushrooms

Mushrooms are fertile symbolic species in Retinal Surrealism. Their caps and stalks naturally support contour rings, concentric targets, prismatic edges, and striped stems.

**Modes:**
- **Eye Mushroom:** A cap with a concentric eye in the centre; acts as a watchful witness.
- **Concentric Cap Mushroom:** Cap uses ring logic and halo edges.
- **Zebra-Stem Mushroom:** Stripe patterns flow down the stalk.
- **Plush Mushroom:** Soft, toy‑like caps and stems for plush worlds.
- **Totem Mushroom:** Large mushrooms used as altar or route markers.

**Uses:** Plant mushrooms as species in landscapes; line them along routes as markers; use them as symbolic flora in plush worlds; mix eye and non‑eye mushrooms. Keep cap and stem patterns consistent with the environment’s geometry.
