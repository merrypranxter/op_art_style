
# Op Fashion and Bodysuits

This branch merges pattern with clothing. Figures wear bodysuits, dresses, stockings, or garments designed from optical systems. Silhouettes become graphic icons and pattern flows with anatomy.

**Core ingredients:** Zebra waves hugging hips and shoulders, contour bands wrapping torsos, radial halos framing heads, target collars, and spoke capes. Garments extend the pattern of the environment.

**Effect:** The viewer sees figures as walking optical artworks. Fashion amplifies silhouette and pose, turning models into living statues. The result can be glamorous, confrontational, synthetic, or ceremonial.

**Best practices:** Fit the pattern to the body: stripes should compress at joints, expand over muscles, and flow with limbs. Tie garments to the world: floor stripes become stockings, horizon targets become collars. Use limited accent colours for glam effect. Avoid generic “striped fashion”; pattern must remain structural.
