
# Spoke Bursts

Spoke bursts are radial patterns built from tapered wedges or blades emanating from a centre. They function as synthetic suns, warning signals, halo frames, or energetic emitters.

**Families:**
- **Hard Angular Burst:** Sharp wedges create a severe sunburst; good for industrial or warning motifs.
- **Petal‑Spoke Burst:** Curved wedges soften the shape; good for ceremonial blooms.
- **Dense Needle Burst:** Many narrow spokes create high tension and vibration.
- **Rotational Burst:** Spokes tilt to induce spin.
- **Halo Burst:** A ring of spokes frames a figure or object.

**Uses:** Use as halo or halo burst for portraits; place in sky as a sun; embed at centre of landscapes; use as caution or warning icon. Choose spoke count and taper deliberately. Avoid generic starbursts by building real centre pressure.
