
# Blacklight Palettes

Blacklight palettes are synthetic, ultraviolet colour systems that evoke nightclubs, ritual theatre, and luminous dreams. They often combine hot pink, cyan, violet, electric blue, white highlights, and black anchors. The feeling is nocturnal, glamorous, synthetic, and energetically charged.

**Families:**
- **Pink/Cyan/Violet Core:** The classic blacklight combination; glamorous and editorial.
- **Electric Blue / Ultraviolet Core:** A cooler cosmic palette with electric blue, blue‑violet, white, and black.
- **Blacklight Candy:** Adds lime and other candy colours for playful but eerie worlds.
- **Toxic Ultraviolet:** Combines violet, lime, cyan, magenta, and black for a more dangerous, chemically bright look.
- **Editorial UV Luxe:** Uses mostly white, black, violet, and hot pink accents for high fashion.

**Uses:** Illuminate halos and thresholds, intensify portraits and glam scenes, energise symbolic objects, charge route markers, and set the atmosphere for club, stage, or ritual worlds. Always anchor with dark zones and strong structural contrast.
